Redesign Capsular from a wardrobe gallery into a wardrobe decision system.
The goal is not to display clothing.
The goal is to help users decide what to wear in under 30 seconds.
Capsular is designed for people with capsule wardrobes who value simplicity, quality, minimalism and efficient decision-making.
