#vision

Capsular helps people with capsule wardrobes make fewer clothing-related decisions.

The product aims to reduce decision fatigue, increase wardrobe utilization and support thoughtful purchasing decisions.

The long-term goal is to become a personal wardrobe operating system that helps users:
- plan outfits
- track clothing usage
- identify wardrobe gaps
- optimize purchases
- maintain a minimal yet effective wardrobe
## Core Principle
Less decisions. Better style.
